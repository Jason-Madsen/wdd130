I imagine he is still working on it and trying to help his junior developers finish on time as well. Lead Developer Report

My experience as a Lead Developer for a website project was phenomenal. I loved being able to get all the work done and portion things off to my junior developers. I had slight issues with content for the site from the client in the beginning, unfortunately he didn't give us a whole lot to run with on the subpages, but we did the best we could with what we had. We used MS Teams to communicate and ensured to update the HTML and CSS after each change so that we could see what changes we needed to make and if there were any big changes we needed to adapt to. Publishing the site was a great learning experience for me for the future, as well as I quickly and efficiently got the link to him so that he could view it at any time. I reached out to him to ask if he wanted anything changed throughout the weeks to know if there were any changes we needed to make. I enjoyed using GitHub and the free resources it has, at first I felt lacking in understanding, however as I continued to learn and experiment I feel I understand now.

General Experience and Reflections

Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed this project and what we have been able to accomplish. While there have been difficulties, I am grateful for the experience I have gained. One of the most challenging aspects was to communicate and work with others who either had not prepared or were very slow to communicate to me what we needed to do. I love to work as a team, however it felt that some members of the different teams did not want to work in the same way that I had hoped. I really loved the work of taking an idea, an outline, and turning it into something real. This made me realize that I could create many different ideas and websites for my own needs and purposes. I think that if I were to do this project again, I would seek to communicate better and understand what my teams expectations are, as well as communicate what my expectations are for them. I feel confident in that I can accomplish the work that is required of me, while I cannot force others to do the work needed from them, I can do my best with what I am given and seek to help them or push them in the right direction so they can do the same.
